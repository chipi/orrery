READING THE PORKCHOP

Each cell asks: "if I launch on date X and arrive on date X+TOF, how much fuel does it take?" Lower numbers = cheaper missions.

X · departure date
When you launch from Earth.
Y · time of flight
How long the trip takes (in days).
Color · launch ∆v
Cool teal = cheap (~3 km/s). Yellow = moderate. Red = very expensive (>9 km/s).

How to use it
Click a cool-colored region near the natural Hohmann minimum (~250-day TOF, every ~26 months) — that's the cheapest launch window. The right panel shows the exact dates and ∆v.
PICK A LAUNCH WINDOW

Tap a cell on the porkchop plot. Cool blue zones are cheap; hot red zones are expensive launch energy.

On mobile, touch and hold the plot to open the magnifier.
