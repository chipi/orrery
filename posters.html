GALLERY · STYLE PILOT

Three candidate visual directions, each shown on the subject it fits best. Pick a column. The full gallery (≈11 posters) gets built in the winner — mixing styles per subject is also on the table.

A

JPL "Visions of the Future"

Bold travel-poster. Stylised illustration, vintage-modern type, saturated palette. Best for present-day planetary subjects.

B

Era-matched per subject

Each poster gets a treatment fitting its era — Mars as JPL ad, Apollo 11 as commemorative patch, Sputnik as Soviet constructivist. Most expressive; gallery becomes a tour through space-art history.

C

Engineering blueprint with color

Technical drawing with callouts, accent teal, mono labels. Extends the /science chapter-cover language. Best for spacecraft + technical-reference subjects.

How to read this: three style directions, each shown on the subject it suits best. Pick a column for the full gallery, OR mix (e.g., A for planets · B for historical missions · C for spacecraft). Once locked, the rest get generated in batch and the footer Gallery link goes live across the app.

Print / wallpaper: SVG scales infinitely. Download-as-SVG + ORRERY watermark are baked into every poster. Right-click any poster → Save as for now; a proper download button comes once the style is locked.
